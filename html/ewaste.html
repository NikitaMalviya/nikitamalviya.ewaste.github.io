Know About E-waste

Electronic waste or e-waste describes discarded electrical or electronic devices. Used electronics which are destined for reuse, resale, salvage, recycling, or disposal are also considered e-waste.

Informal processing of e-waste in developing countries can lead to adverse human health effects and environmental pollution.

Electronics for consumers are being treated like disposable items more and more every day.

One major factor causing this trend is that companies are endlessly marketing new technologies to us, which effectively render recent products as obsolete.
