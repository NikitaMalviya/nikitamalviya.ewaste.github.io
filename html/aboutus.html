About Us

WHAT IS ELECTROTRASH ?

ElectroTrash is a free recycling service for computers, computer accessories and TVs (e-waste).

The initiative is to aim at reducing the accumulation of used and discarded electronic and electrical equipments, which most end up in landfills or partly recycled in a unhygienic conditions by backyard recyclers and then partly thrown into waste streams damaging the environment.The objective of ElectroTrash is to create an opportunity to transfer waste into socially and industrially beneficial raw materials like valuable metals, plastics and glass using simple, cost efficient, home grown, environmental friendly technologies suitable to Indian Conditions.

WHAT ELECTROTRASH TAKES ?

The following products CAN be dropped-off for recycling at ElectroTrash service :

  • Personal and laptop computers and all cables
  • Tablets, notebooks and palmtops
  • Computer monitors and parts (e.g. internal hard drives and CD drives)
  • Computer peripherals and accessories (e.g. mice, keyboards, web cameras, USBs and modems)
  • Printers, faxes, scanners and multi-functional devices
  • All televisions
